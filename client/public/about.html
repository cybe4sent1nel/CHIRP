The Technology

Chirp is built with cutting-edge technology to deliver a fast, reliable, and secure experience:

  • Modern Stack: React frontend with Node.js backend for optimal performance
  • Real-Time: Server-Sent Events (SSE) for instant updates and notifications
  • Cloud-Powered: Scalable infrastructure with MongoDB Atlas and ImageKit CDN
  • Secure: Bank-grade encryption, bcrypt password hashing, JWT authentication
  • AI-Enhanced: Machine learning for content recommendations and moderation
  • Mobile-Ready: Responsive design that works beautifully on any device

Our Commitment to Safety

Your safety and well-being are our top priorities. We employ:

  • Content Moderation: AI-powered and human review to keep the platform safe
  • Reporting Tools: Easy-to-use tools to report inappropriate content or behavior
  • Privacy Controls: Granular settings to control who sees your content
  • Blocking: Ability to block users who make you uncomfortable
  • Verification: Email verification to reduce spam and fake accounts
  • Support Team: Dedicated team to handle safety concerns and disputes
